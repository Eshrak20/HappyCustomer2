Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2, 2020

This privacy policy establishes Common Sense Web Marketing, Inc. ("Company", "we", and/or "us") privacy policy for all of the Company's digital properties and of all individuals who access or use our website(s) including all of its related websites, applications, widgets, dashboards, Happy Customer Reviews mobile apps or business platforms.

1. What Information We Collect

We may collect a variety of information from you, your employees, your business and clients from different devices and from various online and offline sources. If you do not provide information about yourself when requested, you may not be able to use our Services.

The Information You Provide To Us

1) Sign up, Registration and Profile Information

When you sign up for an account, we may ask for your first and last name, email address, phone number, physical address, login information to your online accounts, and any other information we need to establish or verify your identity.

2) Payment Information

When you provide billing information or a credit card number, we use a third-party service provider to collect and store your payment information. We do not store your credit card or billing information on our websites or mobile apps.

2. How We Use The Information We Collect

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, improve, and enhance our Services
  • Communicate with you and provide customer support
  • Personalize and improve your content on our Services
  • For marketing purposes and promotional communications
  • Facilitate transactions and payments
  • For compliance purposes and legal requirements

3. How We Share The Information We Collect

We may share information with:

  • Our affiliates and partners
  • Vendors and service providers
  • Other users as part of our Services
  • Third party applications when integrated
  • Analytics partners like Google Analytics
  • Social networking platforms when you choose to share
  • As required by law or legal processes

4. International Visitors

Our Services are hosted in the United States. By using our Services, you consent to the transfer of your personal information to the United States and other countries where we operate.

5. California Users

California residents may request not to share certain personal information with third parties for direct marketing purposes. Requests can be sent to info@commonsensewebmarketing.com.

6. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please alert us at sales@commonsensewebmarketing.com.

7. Your Choices

You have options regarding your information:

  • Adjust location sharing through your device settings
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails
  • Manage cookie preferences in your browser

8. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, but cannot guarantee absolute security as our Services are hosted electronically.
